Garlic Roasted Eggplant

Description

These melt-in-your-mouth oven-roasted eggplant halves pack plenty of flavour and are a great side dish with just about any meal. The chilli powder adds some extra punch and the parmesan and pinenuts give them some heft. They can easily be made in advance and work surprisingly well cold, too.

Ingredients

  • 3 Japanese Eggplants, halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Paprika Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 50g Parmesan, grated
  • 20g Pine Nuts, toasted
  • 1 tbsp Flat Leaf Parsley, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 200 degrees celsius.
  2. Score the eggplant halves, lightly salt the flesh side and leave the to sit on a baking tray for 20 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile combine the garlic, olive, paprika, chilli powder and a pinch of salt.
  4. Squeeze out and wipe off the excess moisture from the eggplants
  5. Place flesh side down on a baking tray lined with parchment paper and roast in the oven for approximately 20 minutes
  6. Flip the eggplant halves over and evenly spread the garlic oil on them and return to the oven for another 10 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle over the grated parmesan and return to the oven one final time until the cheese is melted
  8. Garnish with the pine nuts and chopped parsley and serve
