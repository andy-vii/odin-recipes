Avocado & Tomato Salad

Description

A wonderfully healthy salad that can complement any meal from breakfast throught to dinner. Creamy avocado contrasts nicely with the crunch of ripe cherry tomatoes. A lemon and olive oil dressing together with some freshly chopped parsley add some extra zing.

Ingredients

  • 8-10 Cherry Tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Avocado, quartered and cut into wedges
  • 1 tbsp Flat Leaf Parsley, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 pinch Salt
  • Black Pepper, freshly ground

Steps

  1. Place the cherry tomatoes and avocado in a suitably sized bowl
  2. Combine the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, black pepper and parsley in a mixing bowl and mix well
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix through
